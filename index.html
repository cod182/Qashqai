2021 Qashqai Specification

Design

Once again, it looks like nothing else

Qashqai brings a new vision with muscular proportions, brilliant LED lighting and massive 20-inch alloy wheels¹. And with more colours than ever before, you’ll be sure to stand out from the crowd.

 

Technology

SMART POWER FOR AN ELECTRIFYING DRIVE

Experience driving thrills like never before with two new powertrains. Qashqai’s mild hybrid enhances efficiency, whilst the future e-POWER² combines electric vehicle technology with the convenience and familiarity of a petrol engine, bringing the instant acceleration, unmatched smoothness and the quiet ride of an electric vehicle.

Smart power for a changing world

MILD HYBRID

The drive for efficiency has never been more thrilling

Efficiency with enthusiasm

Qashqai’s new 1.3L petrol mild-hybrid powertrain features a lithium-ion battery which stores energy recovered during braking to reduce emissions and ensure smoother stop start functionality. It is available with both 6-speed manual and automatic transmissions.
